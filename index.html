Privacy Policy for قطرة ندي

This Privacy Policy applies to the "قطرة ندي" application (the "App"), developed by علاء سرحان.

Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

  • Location data (only when the user grants permission)

How We Use Information

We use location data only for the following purposes:

  • To calculate accurate prayer times based on the user’s location
  • To provide location-based features such as Qibla direction

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.

Data Storage

Location data may be stored locally on the user’s device to improve app performance and user experience.

No data is transmitted to external servers.

Permissions

  • Location Permission: Used to determine accurate prayer times and Qibla direction
  • Notification Permission: Used to send prayer time reminders and Adhan alerts

User Control

Users can deny location access at any time. However, this may affect the accuracy of prayer times.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
